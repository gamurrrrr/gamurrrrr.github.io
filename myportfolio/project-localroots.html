The Supply Chain Gap

The average consumer faces high barriers when trying to buy fresh produce: opaque pricing, inconvenient logistics, and lack of discovery. Small farmers lack the digital infrastructure to reach these buyers.

The Platform Solution

Local Roots is a two-sided marketplace. It simplifies inventory management for farmers while providing consumers with a location-based discovery tool to find fresh food within their community.

2. User Personas

Based on our interviews, we synthesized two key personas to guide our design decisions: The "Time-Poor Urban Professional" seeking convenience, and the "Small-Scale Grower" needing simple digital tools. These personas guided every UI decision to ensure we solved real human problems.

App Interface Design

Simplicity & Visibility

The interface prioritizes minimal visual clutter. The "Explore Farms" (Map) and "Farm Details" screens are designed so users can identify local sources within seconds.

Safety & Constraints

Strict logic constraints prevent user error. The Login system separates Buyer/Seller data, and the Cart class automatically handles inventory reservation logic to prevent overselling.
